16 May 2020

xTheLifex updated:

  • Microwave Emag Feature

09 May 2020

Crossedfall updated:

  • Further tweaks to the automated changelog generator
  • Fixes actions and enabled automated changelog generation

WhyIsCaeciliusTaken updated:

  • Added ability to move and plant mines, as well as disarm them with a multitool
  • Added ability for mines to be "smart" and detect mindshield implants if a particular mine type
  • Clown and stun mines can now be printed in the service and security protolathes respectively
  • three new mine related tech nodes, and three new printable smart mine types for security
  • Added the exploding rubber duck as a 4TC traitor and clownops item, with a more powerful version available for 10TC.
  • Some mines can be ordered from cargo
  • stun mines now do stamina damage instead of a hardstun

08 May 2020

Iamgoofball updated:

  • Conveyors work again.

PowerfulBacon updated:

  • Antags can now have the ability to not delay the end of the round
  • removed confusing and poorly named preference type.

Victor239 updated:

  • Plasmamen helmets no longer have flash/welding protection by default. Instead they have a toggleable visor which acts like a normal welding helmet. It being active can be noticed on the sprite.

07 May 2020

Naksu updated:

  • Cleaned up saycode

PowerfulBacon updated:

  • Special antagonists no longer randomly spawn mid round.
  • Added code to stop events automatically getting added to the event control

qwertyquerty updated:

  • Optimized radiation a little

steamp0rt updated:

  • Guardians/holoparasites are no longer seperated into types
  • Instead, you get to "build" one, with a limited amount of points, selecting different stats and abilities
  • The following major abilities are available to holoparasites: Assassin, Explosive, Frenzy, Gravitokinetic, The Hand, Healing, Predator, Scout, and Time Erasure.
  • The following minor abilities are available to holoparasites: Surveillance Snares, Teleportation Pad
  • Holoparasite types that didn't make the cut: Toy, Lightning, Protector, Dextrous, Charger, Chaos

zeskorion updated:

  • no more random confusion when hit in the head
  • dismemberment now uses a calculation reliant on weapon size, weight, sharpness, and force to determine when a limb is lost, instead of RNG.
  • no more RNG on punches! punches now have fixed damage and no stun.
  • totally changes up block chance. blocking now relies on items being held in the active hand or not, if you are walking or not, and the direction you are facing. Shields now use durability and all other items deal stamina damage to the arm holding them when blocking. Read the pr for more in-depth info.
